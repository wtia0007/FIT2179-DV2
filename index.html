I. Geographic Distribution & Drivers

Malaysia is home to some of the world's most ancient and diverse rainforests. However, our natural landscape is changing rapidly. This story explores the true cost behind one of our nation's biggest economic engines: Palm Oil. By looking at where forest loss is happening across different states and what is causing it, we can better understand the balance between our economic growth and environmental conservation. As the map shows, East Malaysia (particularly Sarawak) faces the heaviest concentration of forest loss, driven primarily by permanent agriculture.
