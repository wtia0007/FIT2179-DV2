Author: [Tian YiCheng] | Student ID: [34402225] | Date: 31 May 2026
Data Sources: Global Forest Watch, Malaysian Palm Oil Board

Introduction: This dashboard explores the environmental impact of economic development in Malaysia. Specifically, it targets policymakers and environmental researchers (Who) to investigate the relationship between forest loss across different states and the national palm oil industry (What & Domain).

By comparing regional environmental metrics against the historical production and average price of palm oil (How), we can observe potential correlations between agricultural expansion and deforestation trends (Why).
